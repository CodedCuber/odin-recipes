Perfect carbonara

Description

arbonara is an Italian pasta dish from Rome made with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork, and black pepper. The dish arrived at its modern form, with its current name, in the middle of the 20th century. The cheese is usually Pecorino Romano, Parmigiano-Reggiano, or a combination of the two.

Ingredients

  • 3 - 4 whole eggs
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 - 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1/2 pound pancetta

Steps

  1. Heat pasta water
  2. Saute pancetta
  3. Beat eggs
  4. Cook pasta
  5. Toss pasta with pancetta
