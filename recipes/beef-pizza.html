Beef and onion pizza

Description

This is a recipe for a basic beef and onion pizza. Iconic pizza from the famous franchise, 'Dominos pizza enterprises'. It is nice and tasty and indulgent. It is made on a pizza base that you can buy frozen from your local superstore for about $3 AUD. The rest of the Ingredients are optional depending on your personal taste and preferences (I reccomend you don't remove the beef though ;) )

Ingredients

  • 10 inch pizza base
  • 500 grams beef mince
  • 100 grams of onions
  • 50 grams of cheese
  • spaghetti bolognase sauce

Steps

  1. Spread spagbol sauce on base
  2. Add cheese
  3. Add cooked beef and onions
  4. Cook in oven pre-heated to 200 degrees celcius for 20 minutes
  5. Best served with chocolate milk on the side
